## 🎯 **WHAT IS GARAJIFLOW?**

**GarajiFlow** is a cutting-edge, cloud-based automotive business management system specifically designed for the Zambian market. It's a complete ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) solution that transforms how automotive businesses manage their operations, from vehicle sales and repairs to customer relationships and financial compliance.

### **Who Can Use It?**
- **Vehicle Dealerships** (new and used car sales)
- **Auto Repair Shops** and Service Centers
- **Spare Parts Dealers**
- **Multi-branch Automotive Businesses**
- **Fleet Management Companies**
- **Automotive Workshops** of all sizes
